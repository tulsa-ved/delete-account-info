How to Delete Your Account in MyApp

You can delete your account directly from within the app by following these steps:

  1. Open the Delibree on your device.
  2. Go to the More Info page.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Enter your password for confirmation.
  5. Tap on Confirm Delete.
Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your personal data will be removed from our servers.
